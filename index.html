Chaturanga Rules & Pieces

The Pieces

Raja (King)
Hathi (Elephant)
Ghoda (Horse)
Nauka (Boat)
Sainik (Pawn)

Dice (Pasha)

  • 2: Move Boat (Jump 2 squares)
  • 3: Move Horse (L-shape)
  • 4: Move Elephant (Rook moves)
  • 5: Move King or Pawn (1 step)

Combat Rules

  • Big vs Small: Small pieces (Boat, Pawn) CANNOT capture Big pieces (King, Elephant, Horse).
  • Friends: You cannot capture your own pieces. You CAN capture your Ally's pieces (except King).
  • Goal: Eliminate both Enemy Kings.
